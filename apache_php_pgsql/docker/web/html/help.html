URL

http://localhost:8110/

Description

A sample php web application with SQL injection everywhere :). Simple sample web application for learning SQL injection. This sample application is a membership site for registering image files and their associated information.

HOW to SETUP

Please read README.md

HOW to USE

* Register your account and login.
  1. Access http://localhost:8110/ on browser
  2. To Register your account, press [Regist New User]
  3. specify username, password, age and then press confirm and complete.
  4. Now your account has created. click [Login] link, then login with your registered account.
  5. MY PAGE is displayed when you log in successfully with your account
* Register your entry, Search your entries.
  1. In MYPAGE, press [Regist entry]
  2. specify subject, contents, mailaddr, imagefile for upload.
  3. press confirm and complete. Now your entry has registerd. press [Return to MYPAGE].
  4. To display the your registered entries, press[Show your registered entry list].
  5. In "your registered entry list" page, you can search entries by specify subject or contents.
* Modify your account
  1. In MYPAGE, press [Modify user]
  2. specify username, password, age and then press confirm and complete.
    * If no password is specified, the application will keep the current password.
* Revoke your account
  1. In MYPAGE, press [Revoke user]
  2. specify checkbox for your confirm and then press complete.
    * *If you revoke your account, the registered entries will also be deleted from the database.
